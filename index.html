A desert is a barren area of landscape where little precipitation occurs and, consequently, living conditions are hostile for plant and animal life. The lack of vegetation exposes the unprotected surface of the ground to the processes of denudation. About one-third of the land surface of the Earth is arid or semi-arid.

Doha Desert

Visitors to Qatar are immediately impressed by the stunning, modern city. With its tall skyscrapers and long boulevards. But for visitors to really experience Qatari culture, there is only one option: a night in the desert. On this overnight tour, enjoy an evening that few travelers experience in a desert camp. Try traditional activities like beach volleyball and shisha smoking. Enjoy a buffet dinner and swim in the Arabian Sea. Wake up at sunrise to watch the gorgeous sunrise with a delicious breakfast.
